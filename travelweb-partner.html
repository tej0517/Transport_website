Type of service include in Franchise

Full Truck Load (FTL)

Full Truck Load (FTL) is the ideal choice for customers who have a large shipment that can fill an entire truck.

  • Cost-effective for large volumes
  • Exclusive vehicle for your goods
  • Direct door-to-door delivery
  • Enhanced security and safety

Part Load (PTL)

Part Load (PTL) is a cost-effective and flexible transport solution for customers those who want smaller shipments.

  • Cost-effective for smaller loads
  • Regular scheduled departures
  • Consolidated shipping
  • Enhanced security and safety

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply for a DLPL franchise?

Any individual, entrepreneur, or business entity with an interest in logistics and operations can apply. We welcome first-time entrepreneurs as well as existing business owners looking to expand.

Do I need prior experience in logistics?

No. Prior logistics experience is not mandatory. DLPL provides complete training and operational guidance to help you run the franchise successfully.

What kind of support does DLPL provide?

DLPL offers end-to-end support, including onboarding and training, operational guidance, technology and system access, marketing support, and ongoing business assistance.

Is franchise mandatory?

No. Franchise ownership is not mandatory. DLPL also offers opportunities for fleet owners and business partners to work with us without taking a franchise.

How long does the franchise process take?

The franchise process typically takes 2 to 4 weeks, depending on documentation, location finalization, and completion of onboarding formalities.
