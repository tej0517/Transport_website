FULL TRUCK LOAD

Complete Vehicle for Your Goods

Our Full Truck Load (FTL) service is designed for businesses that have large volumes of goods requiring dedicated vehicle capacity. With FTL, you get the entire truck exclusively for your shipment, ensuring maximum safety of the material, faster transit times, and direct delivery.

This service is ideal when your shipment volume is sufficient to fill or nearly fill a truck, making it the most cost-effective option for bulk shipments.

Dedicated Vehicle

Entire truck capacity reserved exclusively for your goods.

Faster Delivery

Direct routes with no intermediate stops for quicker transit.

Large Volumes

Cost-effective when you have enough storage to fill a truck.

Door-to-Door

Direct delivery without consolidation or deconsolidation.

Maximum Safety

Your shipment material remains sealed and safe from origin to destination.

Priority Handling

Your shipment gets priority treatment throughout the journey.

PART LOAD

Pay Only for the Space You Need

Our Part Load (PTL) service allows you to ship smaller quantities without paying for an entire truck. Your goods shares space with other shipments heading in the same direction, making it an economical choice for small to medium-sized consignments.

We ensure careful handling at our consolidation centers and maintain regular schedules to keep your deliveries on time and predictable.

Shared Space

Share truck capacity with other shippers to reduce costs.

Regular Schedules

Fixed departure schedules for consistent planning.

Small to Medium Loads

Perfect for shipments that don't require a full truck.

Safe Handling

Your shipment material remains sealed and safely handled from origin to destination.

Multiple Origins

Consolidation from various pickup points to optimize routes.

Flexible Options

Book only the space you need, pay for what you use.
